Non-Genomic Estrogen Signaling Through ER-α36

Promotes proliferation and endocrine resistance in TNBC

Investigated the activity and protein interactions of ER-α36 in non-genomic estrogen signaling. Developed and applied a split-luciferase bioluminescence complementation assay to measure ligand-binding domain activity and crosstalk with ERα and ERβ. Complemented imaging data with proliferation assays, FACS-based live/dead and cell cycle analysis, and western blotting.

SUMOylation of Amyloid Precursor Protein

A steric hindrance strategy to prevent amyloidogenic processing in Alzheimer's disease

Investigated SUMOylation of APP as a strategy to sterically hinder secretase access, preventing amyloid-β production — disrupting the amyloidogenic cascade at its origin and highlighting a therapeutic avenue for modifying disease progression at the molecular level.

Established a preclinical model injecting RENCA cells into mice for in vivo monitoring of B7-H3 expression dynamics. Characterized B7-H3's immune checkpoint function and association with T cell–mediated tumor cytotoxicity, advancing targeted immunotherapies in renal cancer.
